Vegan Bowl

Ok, I'm vegan now.

This recipe is actually from Hello Fresh and it got me into exploring vegan alternatives. It's a refreshing & healthy meal for summer (or any other season if you want ^-^ ).

Ingredients

  • 2 cups jasmine rice
  • coconut milk
  • 1 Sweet Potatoe
  • 1 Avocado
  • 1 lime
  • 1-2 green onions
  • 3 garlic toes
  • 1 cucumber
  • 150g green beans
  • 3 spoons soy sauce
  • 1 spoon sugar
  • optional: 1 chilli
